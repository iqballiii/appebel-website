Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2026

Data Collection and Usage

We collect and process the following information to provide you with a personalized event experience:

  • Personal Identity Information: We collect your first name, last name, and phone number to create and secure your unique user account.
  • Profile Details: Optional information such as gender and date of birth may be collected to personalize app content and event recommendations.
  • Media and Images: With your explicit permission, the app accesses your device camera and photo library to allow you to upload and set a profile picture.
  • Communication & Support: We collect email addresses and any text provided via our feedback forms to respond to support requests and improve app functionality.
  • Device Permissions:
    • Notifications: Used to send timely alerts regarding event schedules and updates.
    • Alarms & Scheduling: Used to help you manage your personal event lineup and reminders.
  • User Choices: We store your preferences for language, app themes, and "Favorite Artists" to ensure your settings persist across sessions.

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Create and manage your user account
  • Provide personalized event recommendations
  • Send notifications about your favorite artists and event updates
  • Improve our app features and user experience
  • Respond to your support inquiries and feedback

Data Retention and Deletion

User Control: You have the right to modify your profile information at any time through the app settings.

Account Deletion: We provide a clear "Delete Account" feature within the application. Upon request, we will permanently remove your personal data and profile from our active systems in accordance with legal requirements. To delete your account, navigate to Settings > Account > Delete Account in the app.

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. All data transmissions are encrypted using secure protocols.

Third-Party Services

We do not share your personal information with third parties for their marketing purposes. We may use trusted service providers to help us operate our app, but they are bound by confidentiality agreements and data protection requirements.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request corrections to your personal data
  • Request deletion of your personal data
  • Withdraw consent for data processing at any time
  • Export your data in a portable format

Children's Privacy

Appebel is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected such information, we will take steps to delete it promptly.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new policy in the app and updating the "Last Updated" date. Your continued use of Appebel after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us using the form above or email us at privacy@appebel.com.
